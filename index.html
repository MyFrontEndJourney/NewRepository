It wasn't until recently that I became interested in web developement. Not having a clue about what any of these languages were, I was intrigued. I signed up for Codecademy, Free Code Camp, and The Odin Project, and completed every one of them (and also spent dozens of hours researching Stack Overflow Q&A's). In a moment's notice, I had become completely thrown into the deep, dark, magnificent rabbit hole of Web Developement - and I knew that there was no going back.
